Introduction to GIS

Geographic Information System (GIS) allows users to collect, manage, and analyze spatial data. It is widely used in various fields such as urban planning, environmental studies, and transportation. This project demonstrates how GIS data can be displayed on a simple website.

Importance of GIS

GIS plays a crucial role in modern decision-making by offering spatial insights that help businesses, governments, and researchers. Key benefits include:

  • Improving resource management by visualizing data spatially.
  • Enhancing urban planning and infrastructure development.
  • Supporting environmental conservation efforts.
  • Enabling real-time tracking and navigation.
  • Assisting in emergency response and disaster preparedness.

Project Description

The GIS website presents geographic information in a structured format. It includes a static map and a data table listing different locations with their corresponding latitude and longitude values.
